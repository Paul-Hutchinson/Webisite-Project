1.6


There are a variety of access issues that can affect a website user who has a disability. A lack of clear or consistent content organisation as well as a lack of graphics can be challenging for users with learning disabilities and memory impairments. To help these users, website can stick to a clear template, or offer and alternate site. Users with seizure disorders may have serious reactions to animations, blinking text or audio. This can be avoided by warning the suer before opening the site, and/or giving the option to turn off those features.
